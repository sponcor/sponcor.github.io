THE GREAT STATE OF ARKANSAS




Overview

"Arkansas? I forgot that was a state!" - You, probably.

Yeah. Me too. I was raised in Arkansas from the age of seven until I moved here to Boulder for school. Though I did not move there willingly, it is where I called home for nearly 10 years. I lived in the Northwest corner of the state in the grand 'ol town of Bentonville. Woo Pig.
